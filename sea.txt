Sea

The sea is a vast body of saltwater that covers most of the earth's surface and
drives the planet's climate and weather.

Tides and Waves
Tides are caused by the gravitational pull of the moon and sun; waves are driven
mainly by wind across the water's surface.

Marine Life
Home to an enormous range of life — from microscopic plankton to the blue whale,
the largest animal ever known.

Coastal Ecosystems
Coral reefs, estuaries, and mangroves form rich habitats where land and sea meet.
Fun fact: the sea holds 97% of Earth's water.
