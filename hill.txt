Hill

A hill is a raised area of land, smaller and more rounded than a mountain, with
gentler slopes.

Formation
Created by faulting, erosion of larger landforms, or the movement of glaciers
depositing rock and soil.

Rolling Landscapes
Grassy hills and meadows often support farming, grazing, and terraced
agriculture.

Wildlife
Rabbits, foxes, grazing deer, and a variety of birds and wildflowers thrive on
hillsides.
